Assalamu’alaikum. My name is Naufal, or you can call me "Naufal FzFr". I was born in Jakarta on March 28, 2003. Currently, I am pursuing my education in the field of Computer Science. Despite having a background in editing such as photos and videos, I have decided to pursue a career in the world of IT, which is full of coding. Although it's quite challenging, I strive to turn this experience into an addition to my skill set.

Additionally, I have also been involved in creating short movies, editing photos and videos, designing, creating Arabic calligraphy art, and more. Sometimes, I also receive requests to work as a photographer or cameraman at specific events. I also have a YouTube channel named "Naufal FzFr". Despite initially aspiring to become a train engineer, I ended up diving into the world of editing, coupled with the world of IT.

Although it can be quite confusing and overwhelming at times, I believe that if we go through it sincerely and with reliance on God, we will receive blessings. Even though I have produced works that didn't meet expectations, I will continue to practice to improve myself. I ask for prayers from my friends so that I can have a successful career and bring happiness to my parents. May all our desires be fulfilled. One of them is "hopefully someday I can visit Saudi Arabia and Japan". Thank you, wassalamu’alaikum.
